Helpful Water Resource Links

Below you will find a variety of resource links ranging from web page listings of other water organizations, to web pages where you can find the latest research findings. If you know of other web resources you'd like to see added to this section please email me your suggestions at darcy.brune@watermatters.org. 

  • Southwest Florida Water Management District (SWFWMD) Free Downloadable Publications
    SWFWMD offers a variety of free downloadable publications covering water conservation, water quality, teacher resources, landscaping, and social marketing resources. It also offers a free water use calculator tool that allows members of the public to calculate their water use. Near the left hand side of the screen, you can filter by topic = conservation. 
  • The Water Page
    The Water Page is an independent initiative dedicated to the promotion of sustainable water resources management and use. A particular emphasis is placed on the development, utilization and protection of water in Africa and other developing regions. Links page.
  • United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) Water Sense Program
    WaterSense is a U.S. EPA-sponsored partnership program that seeks to protect the future of the USA's water supply by promoting water efficiency and enhancing the market for water-efficient products, programs, and practices. This site has a variety of water conservation information and resources.
  • United States Geological Survey (USGS) Water Resources Glossaries
    USGS Resources page provides a variety of glossaries and definitions for water related terms.
  • Water Conservation Basics
    This web page answers basic questions about water conservation, with a focus in some sections on the USA. Suggested by children at the Brenham Community Center in Texas, USA.
  • Water Use It Wisely
    The Water - Use It Wisely website has a variety of water conservation information and resources.
